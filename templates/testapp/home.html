Online Exam Rules and Guidelines

Before Starting the Exam

  1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the exam duration.
  2. Login to your account at least 15 minutes before the exam start time.
  3. Keep your login credentials secure and do not share them with anyone.
  4. Close all unnecessary applications and browser tabs.
  5. Have a valid ID ready for verification if required.

During the Exam

  1. Read all instructions carefully before attempting any question.
  2. Each question must be answered within the allocated time limit.
  3. Once submitted, answers cannot be changed or reviewed.
  4. Do not refresh the browser or press the back button during the exam.
  5. Use of any unauthorized materials, books, or external help is strictly prohibited.
  6. Do not communicate with other candidates during the exam period.
  7. Switching tabs or opening new windows may result in automatic disqualification.
  8. The exam will auto-submit when the time expires.

Technical Requirements

  1. Use an updated web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge recommended).
  2. Ensure your device is fully charged or connected to power.
  3. Disable pop-up blockers for the exam website.
  4. Enable JavaScript and cookies in your browser.
  5. Maintain a minimum internet speed of 2 Mbps.

Conduct and Ethics

  1. Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or unfair means is strictly prohibited.
  2. Impersonation or taking the exam on behalf of another candidate is a punishable offense.
  3. Maintain professional behavior throughout the examination.
  4. Report any technical issues immediately to the support team.
  5. Do not attempt to hack, manipulate, or exploit the exam system.

After the Exam

  1. Wait for the confirmation message after submitting your exam.
  2. Take a screenshot of the submission confirmation for your records.
  3. Results will be declared within the specified timeframe.
  4. Logout properly after completing the exam.
  5. Contact support for any queries or concerns regarding the exam.

Note: Violation of these rules may result in disqualification and cancellation of your exam. All exam activities are monitored and logged for security purposes.

Good Luck with your exam!
